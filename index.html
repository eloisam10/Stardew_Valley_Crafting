Stardew Valley Crafting Calculator

This is a tool where you can calculate all the materials needed to complete the achievement "Craft Master" from the game Stardew Valley, created and published by Eric Barone.
This list has all the craftable items from the game, including the ones from the 1.5 update, so beware of spoilers!
The wedding ring is only available in the Multiplayer and it's not necessary for the achievement, so it's not included.
The material necessary to craft the Crab Pot is not from the Trapper profession.
All the images and information are from the Stardew Valley Community Wiki.
